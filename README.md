## Featured Projects

### 🌱 Que Capim é Esse?

Mobile application developed as part of an extension project at UFSJ to support forage species identification through images submitted by farmers. The application is available on Google Play and contributes to building a georeferenced dataset for future Artificial Intelligence applications.

- 📱 Google Play: https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=br.edu.ufsj.forrageira
- 💻 Source Code: https://github.com/silvajoel/forrageira

---

### 🐾 Veterinary Collective Medicine

Institutional website developed for the Federal University of Paraná (UFPR), focused on the Veterinary Collective Medicine postgraduate program.

The platform was designed to present the course, faculty members, research areas, and educational content in a modern and responsive interface.

- 🌐 Website: https://ufpr.cesmvccoletivo.com.br/
